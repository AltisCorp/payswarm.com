The Goal of PaySwarm

"The PaySwarm web platform is an open standard that enables web browsers and web devices to acquire licenses, collect micropayments and perform copyright-aware digital media distribution."

  • The patent and royalty-free standard would be implemented in all major web browsers.
  • PaySwarm would turn 1.7 billion web browsers into a point-of-sale for digital content (images, music, video, data).
  • Content owners retain all rights and can set royalties and prices at will.

Why Digital Contracts?

  • Legally enforce-able for domestic and foreign commerce via the ESIGN Act.
  • Machine-readable and verifiable.
  • Requires establishing identity of all participants (via banking information).

The Participants in a PaySwarm Contract

  • Content Owner - Provides a license for content (example: personal use).
  • PaySwarm Authority - Monitors and approves the Digital Contract. Distributes payment.
  • Buyer - The individual making the purchase.
  • Seller - The individual providing a download source for the digital content (example: MP3).

Parts of a PaySwarm Contract

  • License - Establishes a Buyer's rights - ie: personal use.
  • Media - Describes the type of media being purchased.
