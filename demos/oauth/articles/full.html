PaySwarm for Content Creators

CLEAR SESSION

The PaySwarm micropayment platform has a number of features that are beneficial to content creators:

  • Easy to deploy into Drupal, Wordpress and other CMSes
  • Great customer experience -
  • Flexible payment
  • It is an open, patent and royalty free standard, which means that anybody can setup their own PaySwarm provider.
  • It is based on open standards such as HTTP, OAuth, Transaction Security Layer (TLS) and RDFa.

This site is a demonstration of the PaySwarm technology. You won't be charged anything to use this site. It is provided merely to demonstrate the customer experience that is achievable via PaySwarm.
